Spicy Pesto Chicken Wing

SERVES 6 TO 8

TOTAL TIME 1 HOUR 45 MINUTES

OIL INGREDIENTS

1 CUP CANOLA OIL

2 TABLESPOONS RED PEPPER FLAKES

1 CLOVE GARLIC

GRATED LEMON ZEST

PESTO INGREDIENTS

1 CUP FRESH BASIL

3 TABLESPOONS PINE NUTS

⅓ CUP GRATED PARMESAN CHEESE (USE MORE FOR TOPPING)

2 CLOVES GARLIC

JUICE OF ½ A LEMON

⅓ TO ½ CUP EXTRA VIRGIN OLIVE OIL

KOSHER SALT AND FRESHLY GROUND BLACK PEPPER

THE WINGS

2 POUNDS CHICKEN WINGS SPLIT AT THE JOINTS, WING TIPS REMOVED

KOSHER SALT AND FRESHLY GROUND BLACK PEPPER

INSTRUCTIONS

MAKE THE OIL: COMBINE CANOLA OIL, RED PEPPER FLAKES, GARLIC, AND LEMON ZEST IN A SMALL SAUCEPAN AT LOW HEAT TO INFUSE THE FLAVOR INTO THE OIL. 40-50 MINUTES THEN REMOVE FROM THE HEAT AND LET COOL FOR 20-30 MINUTES THEN STRAIN OIL AND SET ASIDE MAKE HE PESTO: COMBINE BASIL, PINE NUTS, PARMESAN, GARLIC, AND LEMON JUICE IN A FOOD PROCESSOR AND PULSE TO MAKE A PASTE. SLOWLY DRIZZLE EXTRA VIRGIN OLIVE OIL UNTIL THE DESIRED CONSISTENCY IS ACHIEVED, SEASON TO TASTE. MAKE THE WINGS: PREHEAT OVEN TO 35OF SPREAD THE WINGS ON A RIMMED BAKING SHEET. SEASON WITH SALT AND PEPPER AND DRIZZLE ABOUT ¼ CUP OF THE FLAVOR INFUSED OIL MADE IN STEP 1, TOSS TOGETHER. (YOU CAN COOK THEM IMMEDIATELY OR LET THEM MARINATE IN THE REFRIGERATOR OVERNIGHT). COOK IN THE OVEN UNTIL BROWNED AND CRISPY; ABOUT 45 MINUTES. TRANSFER THE WINGS TO A PLATTER, DRIZZLE WITH THE PESTO AND GENTLY TOSS. SPRINKLE WITH PARMESAN AND DRIZZLE WITH MORE OF THE FLAVOR INFUSED OIL.
